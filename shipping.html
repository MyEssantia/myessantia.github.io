important notes

  • Address accuracy: Please ensure your shipping address (including pincode) is correct. We cannot redirect packages once dispatched.
  • Delivery attempts: Courier partners typically make 3 delivery attempts. After that, package may be returned to us.
  • Undeliverable packages: If package is returned due to wrong address, recipient unavailable, or failed attempts, re-shipping fees (₹60) apply.
  • Delivery delays: While we work with fastest carriers, occasional delays due to weather, festivals (Diwali, holidays), or remote locations are beyond our control.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available on orders below ₹5,000. Additional ₹40 COD charges apply.

lost or damaged in transit

If your package is lost or arrives damaged:

  • Lost packages: Contact us within 15 days of expected delivery. We'll initiate a trace with the carrier. If confirmed lost, we'll reship or issue full refund.
  • Damaged in transit: Follow our return policy — provide unboxing video showing damage, and we'll replace the item immediately at no cost.

📦 Pro tip: Always record an unboxing video — it's the fastest way to resolve damage claims and protects both you and us.

non-serviceable areas

While we cover 99% of Indian pincodes, some remote locations (certain parts of Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep, and extreme North-East regions) may not be serviceable by our courier partners. If your pincode is non-serviceable, we'll notify you immediately and issue a full refund.
