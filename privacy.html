privacy policy

last updated: 6 march 2026

our commitment

At MyEssantia ("we", "us", "our"), your privacy isn’t an afterthought — it’s built into every interaction. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our store or purchase our vetted accessories. By using our services, you trust us with your data; we treat that trust as our highest currency.

Short version: We only collect what’s necessary to process orders, improve your experience, and comply with law. We never sell your data. Ever.

what we collect

To give you the best premium experience, we collect:

identity

name, email, phone

shipping

address, pincode, landmark

payment

encrypted transaction data (never stored)

behavioral

pages visited, referral source, device info

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and Firestore for order history – both are GDPR & SOC compliant. Payment is processed via secure gateways; we never see your full card details.

how we use

  • ✅ order fulfillment: to ship your curated accessories and provide support.
  • ✅ communication: order updates, return assistance, and (only if you opt in) occasional offers.
  • ✅ improvement: analyze which bestsellers you love, to refine our vetted supplier network.
  • ✅ security: detect fraudulent transactions and protect your account.

We do not sell, rent, or lease your information to third parties. Period.

cookies & similar tech

Like almost every modern store, we use cookies to remember your cart, preferences, and to help us understand which products are trending. Most cookies are strictly necessary. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may break (like adding to cart).

Third-party services (Firebase, Font Awesome, Unsplash) may set their own cookies – refer to their policies.

when we share

We only share data with trusted partners who help us run MyEssantia, under strict confidentiality:

  • Shipping carriers: to deliver your order (name, address, phone).
  • Payment processors: to authorize transactions (no storage).
  • Legal obligations: if required by law or to protect our rights.

your choices & rights

You can:

  • access / correct your profile via account settings.
  • request deletion of your data (email us, we’ll comply within 30 days).
  • opt out of marketing emails – one-click unsubscribe.

Because we believe in data minimalism, we don’t keep your info longer than necessary. Order data is retained for 5 years for tax/legal reasons; account data until you close it.

international transfer

MyEssantia ships worldwide. Your data may be processed in India, Germany, or Korea (where our vetted suppliers are). We ensure all transfers comply with applicable laws and use standard contractual clauses.

security measures

We use 256-bit encryption, Firebase's built-in security, and regular audits. Access to personal data is limited to employees who need it to serve you (e.g., support team). While no system is 100%, we continuously monitor for vulnerabilities.

In case of a breach, we’ll notify you within 72 hours as per regulations.

children's privacy

Our store is not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately for deletion.

changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we’ll post the new version here and update the “last updated” date. If changes are significant, we may notify you via email or a site notice.
